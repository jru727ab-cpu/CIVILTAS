What You Do

Every turn, make strategic decisions that shape your civilization's destiny.

  • 🌾 Harvest food, wood, stone, and gold
  • 🏗️ Build farms, mines, temples, and libraries
  • 👥 Grow your population through prosperity
  • ✨ Accumulate enlightenment and advance ages
  • 🔮 Unlock Gnosis for ultimate victory

Quick Start

Clone and run the game locally with Python 3.8+:

git clone https://github.com/jru727ab-cpu/CIVILTAS.git
cd CIVILTAS && python civiltas.py

Then follow the on-screen prompts to begin!

Core Mechanics

  • Resources: Food, Wood, Stone, Gold
  • Population: Grows with food, shrinks with hunger
  • Buildings: Each has unique production bonuses
  • Enlightenment: Path to advancement and victory
  • Ages: 4 progression stages ending in Gnosis

Game Features

🎯 Strategic Depth

Balance resource production, population growth, and enlightenment accumulation. Every decision matters.

📈 Progression System

Advance through four ages as your civilization develops. Each age brings new possibilities.

🏛️ Building Economy

Construct specialized buildings that boost production and unlock new gameplay mechanics.

🧠 Enlightenment Path

Temples and libraries generate enlightenment. Reach 300 points to unlock the hidden Gnosis.

The Four Ages

Your civilization progresses through ages as enlightenment increases. Each threshold unlocks a new era of development.

Age of Survival

0 EP

Focus on gathering resources and feeding your population.

Age of Society

50 EP

Develop social structures and organized labor.

Age of Reason

150 EP

Build temples and pursue intellectual growth.

Age of Gnosis

300 EP

Unlock hidden knowledge. Victory achieved!

Buildings Overview

🌾 Farm

Cost: 10 Wood, 5 Stone

Effect: +5 Food per turn

Essential for feeding your growing population.

⛏️ Mine

Cost: 15 Wood, 10 Stone

Effect: +3 Stone, +2 Gold per turn

Accelerates resource gathering for advanced buildings.

🏛️ Temple

Cost: 20 Wood, 15 Stone, 5 Gold

Effect: +2 Enlightenment per turn

Begin your path toward enlightenment.

📚 Library

Cost: 25 Wood, 20 Stone, 10 Gold

Effect: +5 Enlightenment per turn

Accelerate your civilization toward Gnosis.

Resource Management

🌾 Food

Population consumes 1.5 food per citizen per turn. Shortage causes starvation.

🪵 Wood

Required for most buildings. Base production from population workers.

🪨 Stone

Needed for construction. Mines provide significant stone production.

💰 Gold

Advanced currency for temples and libraries. Mines are primary source.
